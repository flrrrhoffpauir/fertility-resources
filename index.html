There may be times when you have little energy or interest in things that used to be important to you. You'll feel conflicting emotions about your fight with cancer. Sometimes, youll feel unsure about what will happen. There may also be concerns about protecting loved ones. You might attempt to hide some things from them. There can even be times when you feel alone or withdrawn from others.
